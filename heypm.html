HDFC Bank Case

HDFC Bank has decided to launch a banking app for the millennials, to take on the upcoming neo-banks. You have been tasked to come up with a Minimum Delightful Experience for the same.

Required to answer:
1. What are the top user problems HDFC should solve (at least 3)?*
○ How do you validate that these are genuine problems faced by other users also?
○ Identify the impact on User Experience and/or Business Goals
2. Identify the problem you wish to solve (any 1, you can choose more if you wish)*
○ Why did you prioritize this problem over others?
3. What are the potential solutions to this problem?*
○ Prioritize your solutions to address the chosen problem
